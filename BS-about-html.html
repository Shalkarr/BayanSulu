1.About the company

- Kostanay confectionery factory of JSC "Bayan Sulu" JSC "Bayan Sulu" is one of the largest enterprises of the Republic of Kazakhstan for the production of confectionery products. The factory was founded in December 1974 and has been pleasing its customers with the most delicious and high-quality products for more than forty years.

2.Products

- The extensive, constantly expanding line of high-quality products of Bayan Sulu JSC accommodates confectionery products for every taste.

- We offer you a whole variety of great sweets that meet the highest requirements of the modern market. The company produces about 300 types of products.

3.Quality

- Careful control at all stages of production, allows you to achieve excellent product quality, significantly increase its shelf life.

4.Business reputation

- The high business reputation of the company is the key to strong partnerships with major producers and suppliers from Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Germany, Poland, the Netherlands, Denmark, Finland, Latvia, Lithuania, Israel, Holland, etc. An impeccable credit history indicates a high degree of responsibility and integrity of the Joint-Stock Company. The company is a member of the Association of Confectioners of Kazakhstan. The shares of Bayan Sulu JSC are included in the second category of the stock sector of the official list of Kazakhstan Stock Exchange JSC.

5.Sponsorship

- JSC "Bayan Sulu" is the founder of the basketball club "TOBOL" (Kostanay) – a multiple champion of Kazakhstan, winner of international competitions. The company provides constant charitable assistance to orphans, patronizes the family-type children's village "Zhanuya", helps disabled people, low-income citizens, public organizations.

6.Social policy

- We take care of our employees. The company creates the most comfortable conditions for fruitful and effective work. We take into account the wishes of the team and do everything to ensure that a person has the opportunity to develop and improve, support the initiative and in every possible way accompany the professional growth of our people.

7.Competitors

- Healthy competition, in our opinion, is one of the most important factors influencing the development of the market, the perfection of products and the improvement of the production process. Only in conditions of healthy competition can fresh ideas be born, long-term plans be implemented.
