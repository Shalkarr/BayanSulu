About us

-

Kostanay confectionery factory of JSC "Bayan Sulu"
JSC "Bayan Sulu" is one of the largest enterprises of the Republic of Kazakhstan
for the production of confectionery products. The factory was founded in December 1974
and has been pleasing its customers with the most delicious and high-quality products
for more than forty years.
The company is confidently moving forward along with rapidly changing trends.
Occupying one of the leading positions in the confectionery market of Kazakhstan,
the company does not stop for a minute on the way to perfection.
The factory is increasing its capacity, production technologies are being improved.
The close-knit team of Bayan Sulu JSC always adequately responds
to the demands of the time and strives to be one step
ahead of the competition.
